蠶的故事｜詞彙網

This page is here to help you remember and use the key words from 蚕的故事. You can study the words by theme, notice helpful character parts, and practice dictation with stroke-order hints.

What you can do here

  • Study the words by theme group.
  • On a computer, hover on a word to see pinyin and English. On a phone or tablet, tap the word.
  • Learn how 虫、木、忄、扌 can help you remember words.
  • Try the dictation section with English + pinyin, then click Hint for stroke animation.

Learning goal

Try to connect the words to the story: family hardship, caring actions, feelings, and clothing/fashion words. The goal is not only to recognize the words, but also to use them in retelling and new sentences.

Retelling Tip

Try building a sentence from one word in each group, for example:
她們相依為命，所以她很懂事，也靠養蠶過日子。

Or with simplified:
她们相依为命，所以她很懂事，也靠养蚕过日子。
